## Understanding How Points Work

This guide explains how students earn points in the School Fantasy Football League. Points are calculated automatically based on real Premier League player performance every gameweek.

## ğŸ“Š How Points Are Earned

Students earn points for their teams based on the **actual performance of the Premier League players they select**. When players perform well in real matches, your team scores points!

### Points Per Gameweek

Points are awarded based on player performance in each Premier League gameweek:

- **A gameweek** = One round of Premier League matches (typically 1 week)
- **Points update** = After matches are played and official stats are recorded
- **Your team score** = Sum of all selected players' points

## âš½ What Actions Award Points?

Players earn points for various on-field actions:

### Defenders & Goalkeepers
- **Playing 60+ minutes:** 1 point
- **Clean sheet (no goals conceded):** 4 points
- **Goal scored:** 6 points
- **Penalty save:** 5 points
- **Yellow card:** -1 point
- **Red card:** -3 points
- **Own goal:** -2 points

### Midfielders
- **Playing 60+ minutes:** 5 points
- **Goal scored:** 5 points
- **Assist:** 1 point
- **Clean sheet:** 1 point
- **Yellow card:** -1 point
- **Red card:** -3 points
- **Own goal:** -2 points

### Forwards
- **Playing 60+ minutes:** 4 points
- **Goal scored:** 4 points
- **Assist:** 1 point
- **Yellow card:** -1 point
- **Red card:** -3 points
- **Own goal:** -2 points

### All Players
- **Penalty missed:** -2 points

## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can players score negative points?
**A:** Yes! Yellow/red cards and missed penalties result in negative points. This is realistic to fantasy football.

### Q: How often are points updated?
**A:** Points are updated after each Premier League gameweek, typically on Mondays.

### Q: Can I change my team?
**A:** Yes, you can change your selected players anytime. New selections earn points going forward.

### Q: What if a player is injured?
**A:** Injured players score 0 points. Bench them and select healthier alternatives.

### Q: Do old points stay if I remove a player?
**A:** Yes, points already earned stay in your total. When you remove a player, they stop earning new points.
